Twitter Sentiment Analysis

Want to know what Twitter thinks of the new Barbie movie or what everyone is saying about your favorite restaurant?

Well, look no further! This website allows you to enter a topic and receive an analysis on all tweets relating to that topic. The statistics presented in the analysis include the total number of tweets for that topic, the overall sentiment, a few example tweets, a one-liner about all of the tweets, and more.

To get started, simply enter a topic in the textbox below and click submit!
